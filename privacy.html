Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Contact Us

Information We Collect

Personal Details

Your full name, email address, and phone number as entered in our booking or contact forms.

Pet Information

Your pet's name, age, breed, and possible medical conditions as provided in our booking form.

Appointment Data

Your selected services, add-ons, appointment date and times, and any additional notes.

How We Use Your Information

Booking Management

Processing and confirming your appointments, and contacting you regarding changes, cancellations, or queries for additional information.

Service Delivery

Ensuring our staff know of your pet's needs, medical conditions, behavior, and any special requirements before your visit.

Communication

Responding to enquiries submitted through our contact form.

Data Storage & Security

Storage

Your information is stored securely and is only accessible to Snazzy Paws Grooming staff members.

Retention

We keep your private data as necessary to allow informed repeat appointments.

Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or share personal information with third parties.

Your Rights

Under the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020, you have the right to:

Access

Request a copy of the private data we hold about you at any time.

Correction

Request that any inaccurate or outdated personal information we hold is corrected.

Deletion

Request that we delete all personal data is deleted. We may be subject to legal obligation to retain data.

Privacy Enquiries

If you have any questions or concerns about our policies, please get in touch.
